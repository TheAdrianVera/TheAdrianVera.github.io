About

Adrian Vera

Relentless Problem Solver

I am Adrian Vera a Chicago born problem solver currently using code as my primary medium for creation. Growing up I always had a passion for creating artistic pieces whether through sculpture, painting, or music. As I advanced through school I also grew a passion for anayltical thinking, loving my math and physics classes. When I took my first programming course in college I discovered a new love, Computer Science.

For me Computer Science is the perfect combination of creative and analytical thinking. I view the computer as my canvas and the code as my medium of choice for creation. The more frameworks and languages I learn the more I am able to create. I find it empowering because I truly believe I can build anything I put my mind to.
